Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect information you provide directly, such as your name, email address, and password. We also collect financial data you enter into the Application, including expense amounts, categories, descriptions, and dates. Additionally, we may collect basic usage data such as page views and interaction patterns to improve the service.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to provide and improve the expense tracking service. This includes displaying your financial records, generating summaries and insights, and maintaining your account. We do not use your financial data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond what is necessary to operate the Application. Your email address is used only for account-related communications.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers using industry-standard encryption and access controls. We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service. If you delete your account, your data will be permanently removed within a reasonable period. We recommend exporting your data before deleting your account.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal or financial data with third parties for their own use. We may use third-party service providers for essential infrastructure such as hosting and database management, in which case they are contractually bound to protect your data and use it only for the services we request. We do not integrate third-party analytics or advertising services that collect your data.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us at privacy@spendly.app. You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time through your account settings or by reaching out to us directly.
